Hudson's Bay Company Made Beaver Token

Royal Museums Greenwich · RMGC-object-40198 · 19th century
A trade token used by the Hudson's Bay Company, valued in units equivalent to one prime beaver pelt.
Long before Isabella had a face or a number, Canada's economy ran on beaver pelts — this little brass token is the ancestor of everything she'd become.
