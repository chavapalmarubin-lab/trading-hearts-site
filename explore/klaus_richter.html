20 Deutsche Mark Banknote (1948)

Haus der Geschichte / LeMO · numismatik-zwanzig-deutsche-mark-1948 · 1948
An original 20 DM note from the June 1948 currency reform that replaced the worthless Reichsmark overnight.
Overnight, an entire country's shattered economy was handed a fresh start in the form of this note — shelves that had been empty for years filled up within days.

'Bird Dog' Wooden Currency Transport Crate

Deutsches Historisches Museum (DHM) · 1 of 3 surviving crates · 1948
One of only three surviving wooden crates used in the secret U.S. military operation to transport the new Deutsche Mark currency into occupied Germany.
Klaus arrived in secrecy, smuggled in under a codename, inside crates like this one — proof that rebuilding trust sometimes has to start as a covert operation.
