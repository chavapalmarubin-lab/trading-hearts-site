Peso Moneda Corriente Note

Referenced numismatic archives · Peso Moneda Corriente 1827 · 1827
An early paper peso note issued by the Banco de las Provincias Unidas del Río de la Plata.
This flimsy paper note is the first of six times Valentina would be reborn under a new name after her value collapsed — a pattern that would repeat for two centuries.

Casa Rosada (Presidential Palace)

Museo Casa Rosada · Museo del Bicentenario / Casa Rosada · completed 1898
The presidential palace of Argentina, site of countless economic policy announcements including currency crises and devaluations.
Every one of Valentina's six deaths and rebirths was announced from balconies in or near this building.
