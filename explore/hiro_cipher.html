Casascius Physical Bitcoin Coin

British Museum · Object ID 3451279, Collection ID CJE305673 · 2011-2013
Physical brass or silver tokens created by Mike Caldwell, each embedded with a real private key holding actual bitcoin, held today in the British Museum's numismatic collection.
For a currency that was supposed to exist only as code, someone eventually needed to hold him in their hand — the British Museum agreed, and put him in a case next to coins two thousand years older.
