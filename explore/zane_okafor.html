Original Crude Oil Sample from the Drake Well

Drake Well Museum · Drake Well original sample · 1859
The only surviving original crude oil sample from the world's first commercial oil well, drilled by Edwin Drake.
This is the actual liquid that started it all — before pipelines, before OPEC, before a single barrel was ever priced, this was just oil bubbling out of the ground in Pennsylvania.

Referenced via Wikipedia/historical archives · Samuel Kier rock oil ad · 1850s
