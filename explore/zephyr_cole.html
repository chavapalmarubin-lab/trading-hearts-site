Ethereum Whitepaper (Official)

Ethereum Foundation · Ethereum whitepaper, Nov 2013 · November 2013
The founding whitepaper written by a then-19-year-old Vitalik Buterin, proposing a blockchain that could run programmable 'smart contracts,' not just move currency.
Zephyr was never content just being money — this document is the moment a teenager decided currency should be able to think, negotiate, and build entire economies on top of itself.

'Hard Fork Completed' Blog Post — The DAO Crisis

Ethereum Foundation · Hard fork completed, block 1,920,000 · July 20, 2016
Official Ethereum Foundation announcement confirming the controversial hard fork executed at block 1,920,000 to reverse The DAO hack, which had drained 3.64 million ETH.
This is Zephyr's identity crisis laid bare — when code got hacked for $50-60 million, the community broke in two over whether rules should ever bend, even to fix a theft.

The Merge Announcement

Ethereum Foundation · The Merge, Sept 15 2022 · September 15, 2022
Official record of Ethereum's transition from energy-intensive proof-of-work to proof-of-stake, cutting energy consumption by 99.95%.
After years of being criticized for burning electricity like an old furnace, Zephyr rebuilt his own engine mid-flight and came out cleaner than anyone thought possible.
