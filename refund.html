Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective June 26, 2026 · Trading Hearts Productions LLC.

Cancel anytime

You can cancel your membership at any time. After you cancel, you will not be billed again, and your access continues through the end of the billing period you have already paid for.

Refunds

Memberships provide immediate access to digital content. Because of this, payments are generally non-refundable. However, we want you to be happy:

  • If you were charged in error, or experienced a billing problem, contact us and we will make it right.
  • Refund requests made within 14 days of a charge, where the content was not substantially accessed, will be considered in good faith.
  • Annual plans canceled within 14 days of the initial charge may be refunded at our discretion, less any period already used.

How to cancel or request a refund

Use the customer portal linked in your payment confirmation email, or contact support@tradinghearts.com with your account email. We respond within a few business days.
