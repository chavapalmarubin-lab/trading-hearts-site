Aftermath — the hill at dawn, the smoke still rising
"He rebuilt. Within a year, the President's House had new walls going up — the scaffolding visible from the same hill. Within a decade, the Capitol's dome was restored. He was not the same after the fires, but he had always known the fires were coming. Charlotte had done him a favor she had not intended: she had shown him that destruction was not defeat. The two things were not connected unless you let them be."
